The Problem

Where hospitality finance breaks down

Finance teams at hotel groups spend their days reconciling spreadsheets across systems that were never meant to talk to each other.

01 — Fragmented Systems
PMS, POS, and back-office each hold a piece of the truth

None of them communicate automatically. Teams spend hours exporting, reformatting, and reconciling data that should already be in the general ledger.

02 — Month-End Overhead
A 10-day close cycle is not a resourcing problem

Transaction bookings, accruals, depreciation, intercompany postings — each step depends on manual effort. It's a systems automation gap, not a headcount issue.

03 — Delayed Insights
Owners making decisions from days-old data

In a market that moves on RevPAR and ADR daily, a reporting lag costs real money. Operational reality and financial reporting should be the same moment in time.

04 — Slow Expansion
Every new property becomes an IT and finance project

Rigid system architectures turn growth opportunities into implementation burdens — slowing the business at exactly the moment it should accelerate.

How It Works

One platform, every property

Hotel 360° integrates your front-end systems directly into NetSuite — eliminating the manual layer entirely and replacing it with continuous, automated data flows.

PMS & POS capture every transaction

Opera, Simphony, and other front-of-house systems record every booking, charge, and payment at the source.

Hotel 360° applies USALI rules in real time

The hospitality layer processes transactions through the USALI v12 framework automatically — no manual mapping required.

NetSuite GL updates continuously

Every revenue event, accrual, and reconciliation lands in the general ledger — real time, across every property, entity, and currency.

Finance teams shift to strategic advisory

With reconciliation automated, your team's time moves from data entry to analysis, insight, and growth planning.
